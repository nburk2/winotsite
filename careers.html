Food Services Manager

The Food Services Manager will be responsible for managing all areas of operations for Wi-Not Stop Café’s including marketing, estimating food costs and profits, daily decision making, creating and implementing menus, staff support, guest interaction and scheduling, while ensuring the Company’s standards of quality, service, cleanliness and operations are maintained. Must have a minimum of 5-10 years previous experience in a food service setting, to include managing of all departments.
