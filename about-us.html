About

WI-NOT STOP® offers the popular brands demanded by consumers at competitive prices. We operate five WI-NOT STOP® deli’s offering freshly made pizza, chicken, burgers & fries. We are proud to offer Rainforest Alliance Certified coffees and teas (brewed fresh every 30 minutes). WI-NOT STOP offers the world’s finest hand selected gourmet coffee beans with an extensive selection of gourmet coffee blends and flavored coffee in light, medium and dark roasts. All WI-NOT STOP® stores offer fresh fruits and an extensive alternative snack section including health bars and fat free chips and snacks. We offer the most popular cigarettes, beer, phone cards, prepaid gift cards, soda, energy and grocery products in the industry. Quality dairy products, juices and teas make WI-NOT STOP® your “one stop shop” for all your daily needs! Mission

A few years ago, we introduced our first restaurant – the Bealeton Dairy Queen®. Not many like this store are in the state. A sit down dining room with food served to your table. You won’t find this type of service in a fast food chain in our area– other than at the Dairy Queen of Bealeton! The interior décor is classic to the 1950 era and provides a warm and inviting atmosphere to enjoy food from the grill or ice cream for a chill!

Values

WI-NOT STOP® is taken from our corporate family name: E.E. Wine, Incorporated – which is the name of our founder Edward E. Wine.
We are very involved in our community. We support local teams, March for the Babies, MDA, Serve and our local Potomac Nationals Team!
